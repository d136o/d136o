h3. Scaling a Business

* understand why customers buy/use your product?
* don't scale by doing more things

h3. Case Study: RightMedia 

* They built an add network for add networks. But: how do you get people to join your platform?
** go for the losers first!
** the ones that are a little desperate to make some revenue from anywhere
** You won't get the biggest ones, but there's a lot to be made from "the tails" 

* right media acquisition
** built up some tech
** the profit was pure business negotiation and leverage
